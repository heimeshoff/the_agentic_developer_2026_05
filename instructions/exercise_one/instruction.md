# Exercise One

## The Domain

Your team will build a **personal finance and budgeting application**.

The domain covers roughly:

- **Income** — money coming in
- **Budgeting** — planning where it goes
- **Savings** — setting money aside
- **Investments** — putting money to work
- at your own needs and behaviors here...

That's it. No specifics. No required features. No prescribed data model. The shape of the product is yours to discover.

## Instruction #1

**Creatively prompt engineer and vibe code an application.**

Lean into claude. Explore. Try things. See what emerges when you let the model run with a loose brief.
